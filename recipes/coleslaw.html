Coleslaw

Description

This creamy coleslaw features a balanced blend of mayonnaise, buttermilk, and tangy vinegar, complemented by a touch of sweetness. It's a quick and easy recipe that delivers a classic, restaurant-style coleslaw perfect for barbecues, sandwiches, or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ¼ cup buttermilk
  • 2 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
  • ½ tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw mix
  • 2 tablespoons minced onion

Steps

  1. Stir mayonnaise, sugar, milk, buttermilk, lemon juice, vinegar, salt, and pepper together in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Combine coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl. Pour mayonnaise mixture over top and stir until well combined. Chill for 1 hour before serving.
